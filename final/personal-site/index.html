Jingyi Wang

A Digital Media Artisit

I explore natural elements and use them as symbols of emotions and memories. Through the flow of light, shadow, and time, I hope my works can express the tension and transformation of the inner world—revealing the feelings that often go unnoticed in daily life. I am drawn to the traces that emotions leave in our memories, and how they resurface through sensory experience. By creating variations and contrasts within my works, I invite the audience into an immersive state, where hidden emotions and fragmented memories emerge through the layers of light and shadow.
