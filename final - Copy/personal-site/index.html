Jingyi Wang

A Digital Media Artisit

My name is Jingyi Wang. I'm a digital media artist. My initial visit to an exhibition with a friend ignited my interest in art. The exhibition's subject focused on human visual bias, and the artists created stunning and surprising pieces using this cognitive trick. I became intrigued because of the wonder that art gave to me at that time. These variously themed pieces are like windows displaying the artist's intended ideas. Even in the most basic tasks, artists seem to have a tender heart.

To learn more about my artworks, check out my resume. Thanks for stopping by!
