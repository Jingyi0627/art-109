Web / Net Art

Concentration Management

Date: 2024

Medium: Net Art

Description: This piece is called Concentration Management. It’s a Net Art project that visualizes how we experience time and attention during the day. I used multi-ringed shapes that grow and move like layers of a clock or tree rings. Each ring represents cycles of focus and distraction—almost like how our energy shifts throughout the day.

Run Code

Emotional Space Station

Date: 2025

Medium: Net Art

Description: The project is Emotional Space Station. It’s also an interactive Net Art piece using generative shapes and particles. The rectangles and trails react to user movement and gradually reset, creating a rhythm between chaos and calm. It’s about emotion as a kind of digital atmosphere—always moving, but never staying the same.

Photography

Afternoon Window

Date: 2025

Medium: Digital photograph

Dimensions: 8 × 10 in

Description: Soft afternoon light filters through the blinds, tracing quiet shapes onto the wall and furniture. The warmth of the scene evokes a sense of stillness and domestic calm, capturing a fleeting moment between day and dusk.

Apple in Light

Date: 2025

Medium: Digital photograph

Dimensions: 8 × 10 in

Description: A single apple rests in a shaft of golden light, its shadow merging with the warmth of the wall. The image transforms an ordinary object into a quiet study of color, texture, and time, reflecting how light gives meaning to the everyday.

Painting

Crimson Dust

Date: 2023

Medium: Digital painting (Procreate)

Dimensions: 2048 × 2048 px

Description: This digital painting focuses on the human eye as a metaphor for sensitivity and emotion. The soft textures contrast with the vivid redness surrounding the eye, suggesting fragility, exhaustion, and quiet resilience. It reflects how beauty can coexist with vulnerability.

Afternoon Light

Date: 2024

Medium: Digital painting (Procreate)

Dimensions: 2048 × 2048 px

Description: This piece depicts a quiet urban corner bathed in pastel light. The warm hues and layered architecture convey a sense of nostalgia and stillness. Through delicate color transitions and minimal detail, the painting invites viewers to imagine the unseen lives within the city’s calm.
