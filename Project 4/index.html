The disconnection between the emotional world and the online environment

The Internet in the next fifty years is hard to predict, but given the current update speed of the Internet, it will surely still be around people and fill every corner. Perhaps the Internet is no longer composed of a cold screen, but appears in People's Daily lives in different forms and states.

People may not need to start it manually but can awaken it through some medium and record the changes of individuals, groups or even society at any time. The future of the online world can be freely imagined without limit, but along with it comes the fact that emotional connections between people can easily become more fragile because of the Internet. Data is always cold and rational, making it difficult to fully handle complex emotions and unable to effectively read these untouchable inner worlds in a timely manner. This article will explore the possible forms of the network that may emerge in the future and how people's emotions can survive healthily in the virtual online environment.

Data technology

In the future life to come, the continuous advancement of science and technology may lead to a brand-new transformation of network devices. The network will no longer be presented as a screen before people's eyes, but will be hidden in the minute details of life, such as the chips of household appliances, the fabrics of clothing, and the surfaces of high-rise buildings. It will be everywhere and closely collecting people's subtle changes to make the most immediate adjustments. Capture through people's expressions of joy, sadness, or the rhythm of their breathing and the volume of their voices.

To a certain extent, this will make the originally cold and inaccessible technology warmer and more reliable.

When I was tired and wanted to rest, the home appliances had already adjusted the water temperature for bathing and the indoor temperature. When I'm not happy, I will switch to soft lights and play music to relieve my mood. When I have multiple tasks, a reasonable itinerary will be planned for me in a timely manner.

People's complex emotions are quantified by "considerate" online data, and the behaviors resulting from different emotions are explained by the system. People have begun to become experimental subjects that can be effectively analyzed by technology, and emotions have become different research results that are constantly tested to improve technology. Due to the continuous improvement of technology, communication among people will become less and less, and emotions will be quantified into specific data such as percentages and line graphs. All the waves that surged like the sea froze into ice in an instant and never thawed again. The changes of the four seasons were reduced to a winter of snow that lasted for thousands of years in an instant. Those contradictory, flowing and complex emotions were reduced to the changes of data.

Daily life

Communication in daily life may cause emotional disconnection due to the Internet. Family life or living alone is tenderly wrapped by network technology, and every emotional change is well known. Family members don't need to care about your experiences or emotional feelings. Friends will also seldom communicate and share with each other, because people's emotional panels have already summed up everything. The more mature the network technology becomes, the narrower the space for people to build emotions and connections becomes. In the workplace and learning environment, everyone's emotions are transparent and open. People may lose their hidden space, become hard to breathe and survive. Even when alone, people no longer trust their own feelings but first analyze their own changes based on data records.

Emotional space

I'm not trying to stop or oppose the development of technology or the expansion of the online environment. What I mean is that during this process, people should not forget that they actually have the ability to handle information and emotions. People need emotional connections, to share happiness and also to communicate troubles. Emotional space always needs to be reserved by people, allowing them to have the opportunity to think quietly and possess the courage and ability to face others and themselves.
